Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play?
    During WATCH, memorize the sequence. During REPEAT, tap the pads in the same order.
  • Sound or effects are too strong.
    Adjust Sound and Effects sliders on the Main Menu.
  • I bought Remove Ads but still see ads.
    Restart the app. If the issue continues, contact support.
  • My best score was reset.
    Best Score is stored locally and may reset if the app is reinstalled.
