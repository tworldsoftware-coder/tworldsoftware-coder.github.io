Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play?
    During WATCH, memorize the sequence. During REPEAT, tap the pads in the same order. Each round adds a new step. One wrong tap ends the run.
  • Sound / effects feel too strong.
    Use the sliders on the Main Menu to adjust Sound and Effects intensity.
  • I purchased “Remove Ads” but still see ads.
    First, reopen the app. If ads still appear, please contact us and we’ll help. (If your build includes “Restore Purchases”, try it as well.)
  • My best score didn’t save.
    Best Score is stored locally on your device. Reinstalling the app may reset it.
