Privacy Policy – DripLearn

Last updated: January 2026

DripLearn (“the App”) is an educational and organizational tool designed to help users learn and practice structured time-based workflows. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

DripLearn does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Health or medical data
  • Location data
  • Identifiers such as device ID or advertising ID
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

All app usage and tracking activities occur locally on the user’s device.

2. Educational Purpose Only

DripLearn is intended solely for educational and organizational purposes.

The App:

  • Does not provide medical advice
  • Does not monitor real-world medical treatments
  • Does not replace professional training or clinical judgment

Any timing simulations or reminders are provided for learning and time-awareness only.

3. Notifications

If enabled by the user, DripLearn may send local notifications to remind users of planned durations or simulated workflow milestones.

These notifications:

  • Are generated locally on the device
  • Do not transmit any data to external servers

4. In-App Purchases

DripLearn offers an optional auto-renewable subscription to unlock premium educational features.

  • All payments are processed securely by Apple’s App Store
  • DripLearn does not receive or store payment information

Subscription management, cancellation, and billing are handled entirely through the user’s Apple ID settings.

5. Third-Party Services

DripLearn does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

6. Children’s Privacy

DripLearn does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of personal data exposure for users of any age.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
